Weight Gain Exercises

Exercise 1: Squats

Squats are a great compound exercise for building lower body strength. To perform a squat, stand with feet shoulder-width apart, keep your back straight, and lower your body by bending your knees and hips.

Exercise 2: Deadlifts

Deadlifts primarily target the back, glutes, and hamstrings. Start with a barbell on the floor, bend your knees, keep your back straight, and lift the barbell by standing up.
